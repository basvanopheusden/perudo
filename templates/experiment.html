Instructions

This task consist of 100 trials, in which you make a choice and potentially receive reward. After you complete the entire task, you will receive payment for 1 randomly selected trial.

On each trial, you will see a row of images on the bottom of the screen, and a row of hidden images on top. The identity of the hidden images is randomly drawn from a set of possible images. Each possibility is equally likely, and different images are independent.

There will also be a statement regarding all the images together, and you have to guess whether this statement is true or false, by clicking the corresponding button. These buttons also indicate payments associated with "true" or "false". After you click, the hidden images will be revealed, and if your answer is correct, you will receive the associated payment.

Every 10 trials, the number of visible images, hidden images, and set of possible images will change. Before the change, you will see an instructions screen to show the new values and images.
